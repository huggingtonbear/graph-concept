FAQ

View answers to frequently asked questions

How do I download a book?

In the menu on the left (click the hamburger icon above to show ) choose the My Learning option. This will list all books in your reading history on the Mapt online site. From here you can choose to download or remove downloaded books.

How do I get more books in "My Learning"?

Books show in My Learning once you have started reading them in the Mapt online site. please go to Mapt to begin reading more books.

How do I find and download more books?

Currently you can only download and read books that you have already started reading in the Mapt online site. We will be adding the ability to search and discover more books within the app at a later date.

How big are the downloads?

This varies based on the length of the book, and the number of images within. This could be anywhere up to 30MB per book.

What is a Skill Plan?

Skill Plans are made up of Skill Cards that have been curated based on real-world data from over 20,000 surveyed developers and extensive research. Chances are that you're already a professional developer and know the basics, but Skill Plans show you the most important things you need to know in your field to keep up with the market and stay ahead of the competiton, providing the best possible resources to get you up to speed fast.

What is a Skill Card?

A skill card is a unit of focused content which represents a specific real world skill, and contains a curated selection of learning materials designed to deliver the best possible route towards acquiring a new skill. Skill cards help you put our skills in context, and combined with skill plans give you a clear visual roadmap on how to continue progressing for professional development, or simply to grow your individual skills. Completing a skill card contributes towards your overall progression which you can view via the dashboard progress tracker, allowing you to see exactly where your skills are, and where to go next.

Can I change my Skill Plan?

Once you have selected a Skill Plan, you are free to browse the other Skill Plans we have available by accessing 'Skill Plans' in the main navigation. We know as well as you do that the developer market is always changing and that most skills have multiple applications. As a result, if you've already completed certain Skills you may find that you inadvertantly increase your competancy in a different role. The skills you've completed are saved, so you don't need to worry about losing any progress.

How do I use my Free Course Token?

Mapt Subscribers receive a Free Course token every month and can unlock further tokens by using their Monthly Progress Tracker. They can be redeemed in the Courses Library by selecting the 'Buy eBook' or 'Buy Video' buttons within courses. Simply select 'Claim using Token' from the drop menu and the Course will be automatically added to your 'Purchases' within Courses.

What's included within a paid subscription to Mapt?

Paid Subscribers to Mapt can access over 3,500 eBook and video courses on demand. With content from industry leading authors, our content is trusted and practical, giving you exactly what you need to get the job done. Subscribers will also receive a FREE monthly Course Token that they can redeem for any Course in the library, enabling them to download and keep a Course forever. They will also be able to unlock further Tokens by using the Monthly Progress Tracker.

What is the Monthly Progress Tracker?

Our tracker empowers you to make time for learning, letting you visualize your progress - and motivating you to hit your monthly goal to unlock an additional free eBook or video to download. Simply complete 50 sections of Courses and spend longer than 8hrs learning in a month to unlock your bonus Token.
